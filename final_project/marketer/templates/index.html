About

The story

Marketing is a term that covers a whole gamut of activities aiming to make people aware of your products and services and persuade them to buy the same. While advertising is the most common way of marketing, it also involves consumer research, product design, as well as other aspects. However, sales do not fall under marketing, instead, sales are an outcome of marketing. This is why it becomes important to thoroughly understand the importance of marketing !
